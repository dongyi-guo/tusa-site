## sDocuments That Split Across Multiple Pages

Four documents do not map cleanly to one new page. These require the most care during the rewrite.

**managing-membership-rules-and-tags.md** splits three ways: the product-level tag application content goes to §2 Selling Memberships; the product-level restriction logic goes to §2 Restricting Who Can Buy Your Products; the group-level rules and expiry configuration goes to §4 Membership Rules and Expiry. Part 1 and Part 2 of that document map directly to these destinations.

**managing-club-members.md** splits two ways: everything about members themselves (adding, removing, bulk import, juniors) goes to §4 Managing Your Members; everything about promoting to Organiser and the vendor staff limitation goes to §5 Giving Committee Members Admin Access; the roles table goes to §5 Roles and Permissions.

**managing-store-staff.md** splits two ways: the process of adding and removing staff goes to §5 Adding Helpers and Staff; the FAQ comparing staff versus organiser versus owner goes to §5 Roles and Permissions.

**understanding-club-identity-the-keystone-account-model.md** splits two ways: the introductory "Two Sides of Every Club" section (public store versus private group) goes to §4 How Club Membership Works as the conceptual foundation; the remainder of the document goes to §7 How the Club System Works.
