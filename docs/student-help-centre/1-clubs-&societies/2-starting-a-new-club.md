# Starting a New Club

[][1]

Want to start a club at TUSA? Here’s how to get started.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## How It Works[][2]

You can’t just submit an application right away. There’s a process:

1.  **Submit an Expression of Interest (EOI)** – Tell us your club idea
2.  **Meet with a Club Officer** – We’ll contact you to discuss your club
3.  **Get invited to apply** – If your idea is viable, you’ll be invited to submit a full application
4.  **Application approved** – Once approved, your club is created!

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 1: Expression of Interest[][3]

Find the EOI form on the TUSA website under Clubs & Societies.

### What We’ll Ask[][4]

- Your proposed club name
- What the club is about
- Why you want to start it
- Your contact details

### What Happens Next[][5]

A TUSA Club Officer will contact you to arrange a meeting. This usually happens within 1-2 weeks.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 2: Meeting with Club Officer[][6]

This is a friendly chat to:

- Discuss your club idea
- Make sure it doesn’t duplicate an existing club
- Explain what you’ll need (constitution, members, etc.)
- Answer any questions

If your club idea is good to go, you’ll be invited to submit a full application.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 3: Full Application[][7]

Once invited, you’ll need to prepare:

| What You Need         | Why                                             |
|-----------------------|-------------------------------------------------|
| **Constitution**      | Your club’s rules (TUSA can provide a template) |
| **Meeting minutes**   | Record of your founding meeting                 |
| **Member list**       | You need a minimum number of members            |
| **Committee details** | At least a president, plus other roles          |

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## What You Get When Approved[][8]

When your club is affiliated, you automatically get:

- **Club group** on the TUSA website
- **Club store** for selling memberships and merch
- **Startup grant** application for initial funding
- **Access to TUSA support** and resources

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## How Long Does It Take?[][9]

| Stage                  | Typical Time         |
|------------------------|----------------------|
| EOI to meeting         | 1-2 weeks            |
| Meeting to application | Same day if approved |
| Application review     | 1-2 weeks            |
| **Total**              | 2-4 weeks            |

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Tips for Success[][10]

**Have a clear purpose** – What makes your club unique? What will members get out of it?

**Check existing clubs first** – Make sure there isn’t already a similar club. If there is, consider joining them instead!

**Find your founding members** – You’ll need a minimum number of members to affiliate.

**Be ready for the meeting** – Think about your club’s purpose, activities, and who might be interested.
