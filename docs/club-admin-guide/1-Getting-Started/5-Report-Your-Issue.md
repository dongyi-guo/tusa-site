# Report Your Issue

If something on the TUSA site isn’t working as you expect, **DO NOT try to fix it yourself.**

Please send the **five** things mentioned below to the TUSA Clubs team, They’ll take over from there.

The scenario applies to anything on the site:

- A page that is blank
- A button that does nothing
- A form that won’t submit
- An email that didn’t send or arrive
- A product that won’t save
- An order that didn’t show up
- A member who can’t log in.

And many other things.

---

## Where to send it

Email the TUSA Clubs & Societies team: **clubs@tusa.edu.au**

---

## What to Send?

Please include the **five** things below while reporting to TUSA:

- **What were you doing?**
  - *e.g. “I was setting up a new deal for our club.”*
- **What did you see?**
  - *e.g. “The page loaded blank — just the header and footer, nothing in the middle.”*
- **What did you expect to see?**
  - *e.g. “I was expecting a form like the one I use for adding products.”*
- **Where were you?**
  - This means the page address (URL) at the top of the browser, or the menu path you took.
  - *e.g. “tusa.com.au/clubs/our-club/manage/deals/add-new”*

- **A screenshot, please.**
  - **Windows:** Use snipping tool shortcut `Windows + Shift + S`, drag a box around the page, then paste into your message (Ctrl+V).
  - **Mac:** Use shortcut `Cmd + Shift + 4`, drag a box, then drag the screenshot file into your message.
  - **iPhone/iPad:** Press `Home + Power` buttons if your device has a home button, `Volume Up + Power` buttons if your device doesn't have one.
  - **Android: ** Use the shortcut your device's manufacturer instructed, Android universially use `Volume Down + Power` to screenshot.

A screenshot can justify most of things in one go. Capture the **whole browser window** if you can, the address bar and any error messages need to be visible too.

That’s it. Send it through, and TUSA staff will diagnose.

---

## What's Next?

The TUSA staff will handle everything, **please keep the website with it's original error state.**

You can always refresh the page or reopen the browser to see if it was just a momentary blip, but if it is not please be patient and we will get back to you as quickly as possible.

If your task if **time-sensitive**, please let us know while you are reporting.

If the site is **down for everyone** (not just you) or **money is involved** (a payment failed, an order is wrong, refunds), flag that explicitly in your message — start with **“URGENT — site down”** or **“URGENT — payment issue”** so it gets seen first.

For everything else, a normal message is fine. TUSA will reply once the issue has been diagnosed.

---

## Common situations

### “The page is blank”

- Refresh once (Ctrl+R / Cmd+R) — sometimes it’s a momentary load issue.

- If it’s still blank: send the five things. Don’t clear caches or change settings.

### “I got an error message”

- Take a screenshot of the **whole error**, including any red banner, popup, or text on the page.

- Send the five things plus the screenshot.

### “It worked yesterday and doesn’t today”

- Note **what’s different** if anything — different browser, different device, different login.

- Send the five things and mention “this worked \[when\].”

### “Email didn’t arrive”

- Check your spam/junk folder first.

- If not there: send the five things plus *which email address* you were expecting it at and *roughly when* it should have arrived.

### “I followed the instructions but got stuck at step X”

- Tell TUSA which guide and which step. *e.g. “Adding Products guide, step 3 — the button isn’t there.”*

- Send the five things and a screenshot of where you got stuck.
